PRODUCT IDEAS TRACKER
Last updated: 2026-04-07

Purpose
- Keep a running list of product ideas that fit the real business constraint:
  downloadable software or content that a buyer can use on their own, with no
  required seller labor, no required human service, and no mandatory paid API.

Hard filter for future products
- Buyer must be able to download it and use it directly.
- Product must solve one narrow painful problem.
- Product must not require Derek to do custom labor.
- Product must not require emailing anyone, booking time, or waiting on a
  human to deliver the result.
- Product should avoid mandatory paid APIs and avoid required user-owned API
  keys where possible.
- Deterministic transforms, static analysis, local processing, and packaged
  rule bundles are preferred.

Notes for future screening
- Prefer exact version jumps over broad platform automation.
- Prefer one ugly file family over "all documents" or "all CSVs."
- Prefer packaged rule bundles over anything that smells like consulting.
- If a product idea needs hosted infrastructure, recurring live data, or user
  API keys, demote it unless there is a local-first version.
- A product is not "ready" unless it clears the commercial benchmark in
  `docs/commercial-benchmark.md`.
