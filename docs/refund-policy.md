# Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-01

This policy covers paid Zipper Tools digital migration products sold through
Stripe Checkout.

Support email: `support@zippertools.org`.

## One-time migration packs

Contact support within 14 days of purchase if the delivered paid pack
materially fails to match the published supported scope, or if paid digital
delivery fails and cannot be fixed.

Include the order reference, product name, and a short explanation of the scope
mismatch or delivery failure.

Refunds are evaluated against the published scope, limitations, and support
boundary. A repo being outside the published coverage is not a hidden defect if
that limitation was already stated clearly before purchase.

## Preset and rollout bundles

If the delivered preset or rollout bundle materially fails to match the
published scope, contact support within 14 days of purchase and include the
order reference plus the specific scope mismatch.

## What improves refund outcomes

- include the order reference
- name the paid product
- include the exact repo pattern or unsupported finding at issue
- explain the mismatch against the published scope
- include a delivery error if the request is about download access

## What is not a refund reason by itself

- "we decided not to migrate yet"
- "our repo needs custom work outside the published scope"
- "we wanted a full-service migration instead of a bounded digital product"

## Chargeback prevention note

The best way to avoid disputes is to keep scope, limitations, and delivery
clear before purchase. The public site, pricing docs, and support scope should
stay aligned with the exact product being sold.
