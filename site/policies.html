Policies

Clear boundaries before anyone buys.

These are the launch policies for the public site and product. The full markdown versions live in the repo and should stay in sync with this page.

Privacy

The public website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics if the analytics token is enabled. Checkout is handled by Payhip. Payment and payout operations may involve Payhip and the configured payment provider. The public CLI does not send hidden telemetry by default.

Direct support requests send the information you include in your own email. Do not send secrets, production credentials, or regulated data.

Refunds

If the delivered paid pack materially fails to match the published supported scope, contact the seller within 14 days of purchase. Refunds are evaluated against the published scope, limitations, and the delivered downloadable product.

License

The public repo remains under its open-source license. Paid packs are separate commercial digital products for internal use by the buyer's own organization and may not be resold or republished.

Support scope

Product support is limited to published docs, download issues, and clarifying supported scope. If validation fails after a migration, that is reported as a failed migration, not a fake success.

Use safeguards

Use version control, backups, and normal engineering review before applying changes. The product helps with specific legacy patterns and reporting, but it does not guarantee passing application behavior in every environment.
