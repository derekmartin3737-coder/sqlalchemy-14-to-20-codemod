FAQ

What happens to my code?

The scan runs in your local checkout. The public path does not upload repository files. No human review is required for the scanner, fit report, preset bundle, or cleanup pack.

Scope

What does the scanner look for?

The scanner is aimed at repeated SQLAlchemy cleanup patterns such as Query.get, select list syntax, simple string joins/loaders, DML constructor kwargs, and declarative import moves. Risky execution and query-shape cases stay in manual review.
